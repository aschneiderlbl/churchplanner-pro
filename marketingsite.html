FAQ

Common questions

What is ChurchPlannerPro?
A church management platform that brings members, giving, workflows, forms, calendar, facilities, and reporting into one place — with an AI assistant (Aaron) that handles the admin work for you.
Who is it for?
Church and parish administrators who are tired of running everything from spreadsheets, email threads, and a stack of disconnected tools.
Do I have to replace the tools I already use?
No. ChurchPlannerPro integrates with the accounting, giving, and email systems most churches already use, so your existing data flows in. You can adopt one module at a time.
What does Aaron actually do?
Aaron is an AI assistant inside ChurchPlannerPro. It can draft emails, build forms, schedule events, update member records, and run multi-step workflows on your behalf — always with your approval before anything goes out.
Is it really free?
Yes, while we validate the workflows with churches. Early customers help shape the product and lock in pricing for the long term.
How do I get started?
Book a 20-minute demo. We'll show you the platform with your real workflows in mind and get you set up the same week.
